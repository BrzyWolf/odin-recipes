BBQ Chicken Pizza

Description

This BBQ chicken pizza has spicy barbecue sauce, diced chicken, peppers, onion, and cilantro, all covered with cheese and baked to bubbly goodness! This is similar to a recipe I had at a popular pizza place in California. My family loves it!

Ingredients

  • Pre Baked pizza crust
  • Spicy BBQ Sauce
  • Boneless Chicken
  • Pepperoncini Peppers
  • Chopped Red Onion
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Shredded Colby-Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbeque sauce on crust
  3. Top with chicken, pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro
  4. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes
